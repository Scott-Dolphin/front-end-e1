Games


I play several generes of games including MOBAs, RPGs, and some Gachas, but the vast majority of my hours go into rhythm and music games. If you're unfamiliar with this genre, think of games like osu! and Dance Dance Revolution.

Sound Voltex


This japanese rhythm game is by far the game that takes up the majority of my focus. Sound voltex is a Visual-Scrolling rhythm game, or VSRG with a gimmick that focuses on remixing and adding effects to songs. I really enjoy this game because of its low skill floor but very high skill ceiling. My friends and I like to think that out of the 20 difficulty levels, the first twelve considered "tutorial" levels, and the actual game begins at level 15. For reference, I only recently started clearing lvl 17 charts this August
